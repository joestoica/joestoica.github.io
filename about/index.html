About Me

Hi! My name is Joe Stoica. I’m a graduate student from Indiana University pursuing a master’s degree in statistical science. This coming summer, I’ll be interning at Cook Medical, where I’ll be a statistics intern in their Quality Engineering department. This previous summer I was the media analytics intern at MullenLowe Mediahub, where I assisted in data analysis and reporting for advertising campaigns.

I’ve previously worked at Upland Brewing Co., where I compiled and analyzed supply chain data, and with the Cognitive Development Lab at IU, where I completed a semester long analysis project regarding the pathways for how children learn the mathematical concept of place value. Have a closer look at some of the things I’ve worked on or feel free to contact me if you would like to see my resume.

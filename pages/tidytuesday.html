What is
Tidy Tuesday?

Tidy Tuesday is a data project hosted every week by the R for Data Science learning community. Anyone is welcome to participate, and are encouraged to colloborate and share their findings on Twitter with the R4DS community. This page contains my R markdowns for my submissions!

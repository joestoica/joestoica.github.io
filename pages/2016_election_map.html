About Me

I am currently a senior at Indiana University pursuing a degree in Statistics, with minors in Mathematics and Biology. I enjoy exploring new data sets and analyzing them using statistical methods. However, my primary motivation is trying to uncover the underlying story that the data is hiding and share these stories in a publicly accessible and easy to understand way.
